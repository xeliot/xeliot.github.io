Eemergency

The main goal of (E)emergency is to alert vehicles that there is an emergency vehicle approaching and they will be required to pull over in a timely manner. (E)emergency employs technology that uses the emergency vehicles location to send alerts to the vehicles that may be affected in the path ahead. This alert gives the vehicles that are ahead, an alert style notification, similar to an amber alert. Our goal is to get emergency responders to the scene as fast, and as safe as possible to keep the public safe as possible. Seconds can save lives, our goal at (E)emergency is to provide our first responders every tool they need to do their jobs as effective as possible.


Filed three provisional patents on emergency repsonse system architecture.

  • Scalable Real Time in Memory Storage and Indexing for Emergency Response System
